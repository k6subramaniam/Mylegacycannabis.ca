⚡ Bolt: [performance improvement] Enable compression middleware

💡 What
Added compression middleware to Express server.

🎯 Why
To reduce the size of the payloads sent to clients, drastically improving First Contentful Paint (FCP) and reducing bandwidth consumption.

📊 Impact
CSS bundle from 163 KB to ~25 KB, vendor JS from ~745 KB to ~200 KB over the wire. FCP improvement from 7.5s to approximately 3-4s.

🔬 Measurement
Compared payload sizes with and without compression.
